About

I am now a first-year MS ECE student at UCLA. I obtained my Bachelor degree in Automation from Tongji University in 2021.

Before enrolling in UCLA, I worked as a research assistant supervised by Prof. Xiang Li in Shanghai Research Institute for Intelligent Autonomous Systems in 2021 and 2022.

My research interests include game theory, reinforcement learning, networked systems, control theory and graph theory.
