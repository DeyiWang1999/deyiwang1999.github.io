About me

I am now a PhD student at the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, University of Michigan, Ann Arbor advised by Prof. Lei Ying. I obtained my bachelor's degree in Automation from Tongji University in 2021 and my master's degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from University of California, Los Angeles in 2024. From 2021 to 2022, I worked as a research assistant supervised by Prof. Xiang Li at Shanghai Research Institute for Intelligent Autonomous Systems.
